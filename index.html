WORK

Whether you're a freelancer, entrepreneur, telecommuter, or someone who just needs access to an office, OPEN DOOR COWORKING has a spot for you. Coworking is for anyone who needs a desk or a place to meet with clients that isn't a coffee shop or a kitchen table.

COLLABORATE

One of the best things about a coworking space is the diversity of the membership. Coworking is not just for programmers and designers anymore. It's for lawyers, CPAs, photographers, and anyone else. Putting such an assortment of backgrounds into a single area often results in COLLABORATION that may not have otherwise had the chance to happen.

INNOVATE

Putting so many diverse minds together not only increases collaboration, but INNOVATION as well. While members of a coworking space may all be from different companies or have different purposes for being there, opening the lines of communication between different fields opens up endless possibilities of what can come out of it.
